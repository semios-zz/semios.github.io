About Us

Semios is an emerging leader in the development of M2M technology for agriculture. Our technology enables growers of high-value crops to monitor for insect pests, plant diseases and micro-climates and determine where and when to best take action to protect and increase crop value. We are at the forefront of a new wave of agricultural technology innovation involving precision agriculture, biological pest control and data management.
