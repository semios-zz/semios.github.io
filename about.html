Our Mission

Semios is an emerging leader in the development of M2M technology for agriculture. Our technology enables growers of high-value crops to monitor for insect pests, plant diseases and micro-climates and determine where and when to best take action to protect and increase crop value. We are at the forefront of a new wave of agricultural technology innovation involving precision agriculture, biological pest control and data management. The company headquarters are in Vancouver, British Columbia with technical staff at the Vineland Research Centre in Ontario and business development staff located in the Netherlands.

The company was founded by Dr. Michael Gilbert in Vancouver, Canada.

Protect and Increase the Value of Your Apples, Pears and Peaches.

Semios delivers precision data and intelligence for daily decision making that increases crop value.

Meet The Team

  • Dr. Michael GilbertFounder & CEO

    Michael has 20 years of experience as a scientist and manager in bitoech R&D, including Merck and and Cardiome Pharma. After Cardiome exited their main asset for $800M, he founded semios to meet the growing demand for sustainable and traceable food production with precision crop data and intelligence. Michael is a natural product synthetic chemist by training with his PhD from UBC and a post-doctoral fellowship at University of Vienna.

  • Chris Van Natto, MSVP of Product Development

    Mr. Van Natto has more than 22 years of experience in agriculture with the federal government, DuPont Canada, and several start-up ventures. He has worked in R&D, regulatory affairs, and commercialization for products and has vast experience coordinating the activities of various contract researchers, university, and government collaborators in support of regulatory and product launch activities.

  • Len MetcalfeCTO

    Mr. Metcalfe was Chairman and CEO of Dynamic Control Systems Inc. a manufacturer of applied laser sensor and machine vision technology from 1976-1997. In 1997 Dynamic Control Systems acquired several international sensor manufacturers and became LMI Technologies Inc. Mr. Metcalfe remained Chairman, CEO until 2009 when he retired from the CEO position and remains a Director after LMI was acquired by AUGUSTA Technologie AG. in 2011. Mr Metcalfe is an Independent Director of WebTech Wireless Inc. a public company and also a director of several private companies. Mr Metcalfe is currently an active Entrepreneur, Advisor, Director and Angel Investor.
